# Rasa - Customer Discovery Field Kit

Use this to validate the PRD before writing more code.

## Sprint

- Duration: 10 days
- Audiences: creators, restaurants, users
- Decision day: Day 11
- Rule: compliments do not count; only commitments count.

## Mom Test Rules

1. Talk about their life, not your idea.
2. Ask about specific past behavior, not generic future opinions.
3. Talk less, listen more.

High signal:

- Specific past behavior
- Money already spent
- Workarounds already built

Low signal:

- "Great idea"
- "I would use it"
- "I know people who would want this"

## Creator Sprint

- Target: 12-15 Hyderabad food creators
- Threshold: 5 firm yeses to a 6-month platform-priority agreement
- Critical commitment: one live fest per month, tag Rasa in top 4 organic posts, 7% net booking revenue plus ₹15,000 minimum per live fest.

## Restaurant Sprint

- Target: 8-10 mid-to-premium restaurants
- Threshold: 3 yeses to ₹2,499/month SaaS and 5 yeses to monthly live fests
- Critical commitment: written 90-day pilot agreement with booking/POS data access.

## User Sprint

- Target: 15-20 Hyderabad professionals
- Threshold: 50%+ real yes conversion
- Critical commitment: immediate founding-user pre-registration.

## Day 10 Rubric

| Pillar              | Pass Criterion                                 | Status |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------ |
| Creator Pillar      | 5+ firm yeses with willingness to sign         | /      |
| Restaurant Pillar   | 3+ SaaS yeses and 5+ live fest yeses           | /      |
| User Pillar         | 50%+ real-yes conversion                       | /      |
| Authenticity Thesis | 7+ creators describe trust collapse unprompted | /      |
| Live Fest Thesis    | 5+ restaurants confirm slow-slot pain          | /      |

## Day 11 Decision

- 5/5 pass: build Phase 0.
- 3-4/5 pass: build strong pillars, rethink weak ones.
- 2/5 pass: pause and structurally pivot.
- 0-1/5 pass: stop.
